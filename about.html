My Journey

I started my career with a Bachelor’s in Computer Science and spent 1.5 years as a Systems Engineer at a Fortune 500 company. While pursuing my postgrad in Business and Fintech, I discovered a passion for design and wanted to dive deep into development to bring my designs and concepts to life. This led me to join a software engineering bootcamp, one of the best decisions I’ve ever made. Now, I’m excited to contribute and grow as a software engineer, ready to tackle new challenges and innovate in the tech world.

About
